Eligibility Criteria

  1. candidate must have completed their graduation with a minimum of 50% marks (45% for sc/st category) or an equivalent cgpa in any discipline from a recognized university.
  2. candidates currently in their final year of graduation can also apply. in that case, the admission offered will be provisional till the time graduation documents are not received.
  3. candidates must have appeared in any one of the following entrance exams - cat, mat, cmat, atma,xat, gmat and state entrances.
